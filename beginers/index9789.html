Exploring Consumer Preferences and Perceptions towards Sustainable Clothing Brands

Abstract

This research aims to delve into consumer preferences and perceptions regarding sustainable clothing brands. With increasing environmental consciousness and concerns about ethical sourcing in the fashion industry, understanding consumer attitudes towards sustainable clothing brands becomes paramount. Through a comprehensive literature review and empirical research methods such as surveys and interviews, this study seeks to identify the factors influencing consumers' decisions when purchasing clothing items, particularly focusing on sustainability aspects. The findings of this research can provide valuable insights for clothing brands aiming to enhance their sustainability initiatives and effectively target environmentally-conscious consumers.
